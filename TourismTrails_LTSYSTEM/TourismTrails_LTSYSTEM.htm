Description:
Abstract:
The LTSYSTEM data layer includes all trails within the Long Trail (LT) System. The LTSYSTEM layer also includes the portion of the Appalachian Trail (AT) within Vermont. The Green Mountain Club (GMC) manages the Long Trail System. The LTSYSTEM layer was developed by the GMC from a variety of sources
Purpose:
To depict the location and extent of all trails within the Long Trail System.
